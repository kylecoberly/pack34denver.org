Cub Scout Pack 34

Denver, CO

We are a large and active pack serving families (both boys and girls) in the general East Denver area. Cub Scouts spans from Kinder thru 5th grade. Our pack welcomes all age-eligible youth -- no matter their gender, culture, ethnicity, socioeconomic status, religion, ability, family background, or family structure -- who'll "do their best" to follow the Scout Oath and Law. You can learn more about Cub Scouts through the official Aims & Methods, and Family Information Guide.

Contact us at pack34@gmail.com.

Meetings and Events

Pack 34 meets at the Bill Roberts Elementary School cafeteria/field at 6:15pm on Monday evenings. The pack meets as large group approximately once a month, and as smaller dens most weeks. Additionally, the pack organizes campouts, cookouts, and other activities throughout the year.

Uniform and materials

Uniforms can be purchased at scoutshop.org or in-person at the Scout Shop in Lakewood. The scout shop can sew the patches for you on-site and they also sell shirts with some patches pre-sewn.

Lions

  • Lion T-Shirt
  • Lion scout/parent book set
  • Lion neckerchief and slide
  • Lion hat ($17.99, optional)
  • Scout belt ($9.99, optional)

All required Lion materials are included with Pack 34 membership dues.

Tigers, Wolves, and Bears

  • Official blue Cub Scout shirt ($29.99) with required patches (World Crest patch, Denver Area Council patch, "3" and "4" numerals in red and white- $8.87)
  • Either:
    • Official blue Cub Scout skort ($19.99)/shorts ($12.99)/pants ($24.99)/capris ($24.99)
    • Similarly colored pants in another brand
  • Den-specific handbooks, neckerchiefs, and slides (included with Pack 34 membership dues).
  • Blue Cub Scout belt ($9.99, optional)
  • Cub scout socks ($6.99, optional)
  • Den-specific hat ($17.99, optional)

Webelos & Arrow of Lights

  • Official tan Scouts BSA shirt ($22-38) with required patches (World Crest patch, Denver Area Council patch, "3" and "4" numerals in tan and green, and blue shoulder loops- $13.76)
  • Either:
    • Official green Scouts BSA skort ($24.99)/shorts ($19.99+)/pants ($34.99+)/capris ($44.99)
    • Similarly colored pants in another brand
  • Den-specific handbooks, sleeve ribbons, neckerchief, and slides (included with Pack 34 membership dues).
  • Green Scouts BSA belt ($12.99)
  • Scouts BSA socks ($8.99+, optional)
  • Den-specific hat ($19.99, optional)
